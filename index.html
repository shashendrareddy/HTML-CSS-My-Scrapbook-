Things I Love Most in Life

Favourite Games

Foot Ball

Football, also known as soccer, is the world’s most popular sport, played and watched by millions across the globe. It is a fast-paced game that requires teamwork, strategy, and skill, making it both exciting to play and thrilling to watch. The sport brings people together, inspires passion, and teaches valuable lessons about discipline and perseverance.


TUG OF WAR

Tug of War is a traditional team sport that tests strength, teamwork, and coordination. Two teams pull on opposite ends of a rope, each trying to drag the other team across a central line. The game is simple yet exciting, encouraging cooperation and friendly competition. Tug of War is often played at school events, festivals, and community gatherings, bringing people together for fun and physical activity.


HOCKEY

Hockey is a fast-paced and exciting team sport played on a field or ice, where players use sticks to hit a ball or puck into the opponent’s goal. It requires speed, skill, teamwork, and strategy. Hockey is popular worldwide and is known for its thrilling gameplay, quick passes, and energetic atmosphere. Playing hockey helps improve coordination, fitness, and fosters a strong sense of camaraderie among teammates.

WALKING IN MORNING

Morning walks, especially on an empty stomach, are said to burn more fat and energise the heart by kickstarting circulation early in the day.
Research supports that exercising in the morning can improve adherence to a routine, people tend to stick to morning schedules more than evening ones.

Bike Riding

Bike riding is a fun and healthy activity that offers both adventure and exercise. It helps improve cardiovascular fitness, strengthens muscles, and boosts mental well-being. Whether enjoyed as a sport, a hobby, or a means of transportation, cycling allows you to explore new places, enjoy the outdoors, and experience a sense of freedom on the open road.


Favourite Food Items.

Biryani

Biryani is a quintessential Indian dish, a symphony of flavors and textures where fragrant basmati rice meets succulent marinated meat (or vegetables),
all layered with aromatic spices and often adorned with fried onions, fresh herbs, and a drizzle of ghee.

Lemon Rice

Lemon rice is a popular South Indian dish known for its tangy and refreshing flavor. Made with cooked rice, fresh lemon juice, turmeric, and a tempering of mustard seeds, curry leaves, and peanuts, it is both simple and delicious. Lemon rice is often enjoyed as a light meal or packed lunch, and its vibrant taste makes it a favorite comfort food for many.



Tomato Curry

Tomato curry is a flavorful and comforting dish made with ripe tomatoes, onions, and a blend of aromatic spices. This curry is easy to prepare and is enjoyed with rice or Indian breads like roti and chapati. The tangy taste of tomatoes combined with spices creates a delicious and satisfying meal that is loved by many for its simplicity and rich flavor.
